Meta tags

Meta tags are html tags that are placed in the head of the document.

There are two very important, high SEO impact tags that you must attend to, and that it is the title tag and the description tag.

These two are also the most important marketing tags. These are the two tags that appear in the SERP (search engine results page). They are the two texts that a user will see before they decide to click into your website.

In the SERP page, google normally displays the URL of the website, your title and your description.


Title

The title should not exceed 70 characters, SEO tools do not approve anything above that. Firstly any text above that will be cut off and secondly there might be a small penalty incurred for not following length guidelines.

Ensure your most important ranking keyword is in the title, if it is possible try and ensure it is the first word in the title.

The title is not the name of the website, the title should be what the page is about.

It follows that each webpage should have a separate and genuine title so it wont confuse google's index.

Make a catchy title. Make a title that will grab the attention and curiosity of the web user.

Description tag

The description tag should not exceed 160 characters

Author tag

This is the tag, where you inform the search engine who the author, designer, of the website. You can put a person's name and organisation. I am not sure if it can be filled with multiple authors.

Keywords tag

There is some debate with SEO specialists on the importance of this meta tag. Some say, they have no value and have no impact on SEO and others say there is no harm in using them.

I think you can use them, just ensure you do not duplicate keywords.

In the past Google penalized websites that stuffed keywords into this meta tag.
