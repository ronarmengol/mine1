WHAT IS A WEBSITE

A website is just a compilation of files that anyone on the internet can access.

Publishing a website will not find customers for you.

You help customers find you by investing in search engine optimization(SEO), and having consistent research on your market and competition and a digital marketing strategy (online visibility).
